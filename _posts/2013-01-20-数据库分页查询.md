--Oracle

关键字rownum

--按照入职时间的顺序，查询从第7个到第10个人  

```
select t2.*
  from (select t1.*, rownum rn
          from (select * from emp order by hiredate) t1
         where rownum <= 10) t2
 where rn >= 7;

```
